PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE

Take Care of Your Computer

Simple maintenance habits can help prevent hardware problems, improve performance, and extend the life of your computer.

01

Keep It Clean

Remove dust from your computer, fans, and air vents to maintain proper airflow.

Clean dust regularly
02

Prevent Overheating

Make sure your computer has enough airflow and avoid blocking ventilation areas.

Keep air vents clear
03

Protect From Power Issues

Power surges and sudden outages can damage important computer components.

Use a surge protector
04

Handle Components Carefully

Computer components are sensitive to physical damage and static electricity.

Handle parts by the edges
05

Protect Your Storage

Avoid physical shocks and keep important files backed up to prevent data loss.

Back up important files
06

Perform Regular Maintenance

Check your computer regularly to find possible problems before they become serious.

Inspect your hardware

Remember: Prevention is better than repair.

A few minutes of regular maintenance can help your computer stay reliable for years.
